THE STATE OF DO NOT TRACK IN FIREFOX


Do Not Track is a step toward putting you in control of the way your information is collected and used online and is a feature in Firefox that allows you to let a website know you would like to opt-out of third-party tracking for purposes including behavioral advertising. No Firefox user was tracked to generate this data. Please refer to the Do Not Track FAQ on our website for more info.
